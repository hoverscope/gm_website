GreenMiles uses GPS tracking and transport mode detection to automatically recognize when you're walking, cycling, using public transport, or carpooling. You earn points for each sustainable mile traveled, with bonus multipliers for longer journeys and consistent eco-friendly choices.

Trade your points for discounts at local cafés, grocery stores, and eco-friendly retailers. Collect limited-time digital badges, unlock exclusive merchandise, and participate in seasonal reward drops tied to holidays and cultural events.
